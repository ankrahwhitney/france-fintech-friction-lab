## Sensitivity

The checklist wins 4 of 5 weight profiles (Balanced, Growth first, Operations first, Evidence
first). **Speed first selects the explainer** (77.8 vs 74.6). If leadership optimises for the
fastest learning cycle, the recommendation changes — that dependency is surfaced, not hidden.

## The decision

Run a controlled test of the checklist: primary metric document submission (baseline 57.1%,
MDE 2.5 pp, 6,103 per arm, 12,206 total ≈ 37 days at 10,000 applications/month), funded-in-7-days
as directional secondary, guardrails on verification pass rate, manual-review load and latency,
support contact and compliance indicators. Advance only if the primary effect is statistically
positive, its point estimate is at least 1.5 pp and no guardrail breaches its tolerance.
